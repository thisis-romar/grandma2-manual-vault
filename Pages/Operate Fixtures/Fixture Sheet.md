# Fixture Sheet

The fixture sheet is where your attributes with their corresponding values are located. Moreover, you can select fixtures here. 

**Requirement:**

*   Fixtures are patched.

Patch fixtures in **Setup – Patch & Fixture Schedule**. 

For more information on how to patch fixtures see [Adding fixtures to your show](../Patching/Adding%20fixtures%20to%20the%20show.md).

![](../../img/img_edit-setup_patch-fixtures_v3-3.png)

_Edit Setup – Patch Fixtures_

**Important:**  
It is possible to assign both – fixtures and channels. Hence, fixtures are displayed in their corresponding sheets depending on what ID you have assigned – **fixture ID or channel ID**.

To open the fixture sheet:

1.  Tap anywhere in an empty screen.
2.  The pop-up **Create Basic Window** opens. 

![](../../img/popup_createbasicwindow_3_2.png)

_Pop-up Create Basic Window_

3.  Select the category **Sheets** in the tabs. 
4.  Tap **Fixture**. 
5.  The fixture sheet opens.

This sheet contains all fixtures and fixture types with a fixture ID. 

![](../../img/image_fixture-sheet-view_v3-3.png)

_Fixture Sheet_

**Important:**  
The title bar displays the world name and ![](../../img/icon_world_v3-2.png) if you are in a world. Furthermore, it displays the word "Mask" and ![](../../img/icon_ghost_v3-2.png) if a mask was applied. 

For general information on worlds see [What are worlds](../Worlds%20Filters%20and%20Masks/What%20are%20Worlds.md). 

For general information on masks see [What are masks](../Worlds%20Filters%20and%20Masks/What%20are%20masks.md).

The fixture sheet also consists of different colors, markers and background colors. For more information see [Marker](../Workspace/Marker%20Colors.md) and [System](../Workspace/System%20Colors.md). 

* * *

## Layers

The fixture sheet also comprises a bar of layers, which is located at the bottom of the fixture sheet.

For more information on each layer see [Layers in sheets – Channel sheet and fixture sheet](Channel%20Sheet%20and%20Fixture%20Sheet.md).

* * *

## Options

To open the options in the fixture sheet, tap ![](../../img/icon_yellow-ball_3_2.png)in the upper left corner of the sheet.

The dialog **Fixture Sheet** **Options** opens. 

![](../../img/window_fixture-sheet-options_v3-3.png)

_Fixture Sheet Options_

To learn about the single options of the fixture sheet see:

*   [Tools](Tools.md) 
*   [Layer mask](Layer%20mask.md)
*   [Display](Display.md)
*   [Title buttons](Title%20buttons.md)
*   [Assign executor](Assign%20executor.md)
*   [Mask (local)](Mask%20%28local%29.md)

To close the options, tap ![](../../img/icon_close_small_v3-2.png) in the upper right corner of the dialog. 

* * *

## Close the Fixture Sheet

To close the fixture sheet altogether, tap ![](../../img/icon_yellow-ball_3_2.png) in the upper left corner of the window and tap Delete Window.
