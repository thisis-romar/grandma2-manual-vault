# Network Dimmer

Monitor and configure a dimMA system in the window **MA Dimmer Network**. 

**Important:**  
This section deals with the configuration of MA NDPs in the view **MA Dimmer Network**. The configuration of MA NDPs in the window **MA Network Configuration** is dealt with in [[Pages/Control MA NDPs/Add the MA NDPs to the Network Configuration|Control the MA NDP]]. 

**Requirement:**

*   Add MA NDPs to the network configuration

For more information see [[Pages/Networking/Adding devices to the session|Adding devices to your session]].

1.  Open the **MA Dimmer Network**.  
    \-Open **Create Basic Window**. For more information see [[Sections/Windows|Manage windows]].  
    \-Tap System and then tap Network Dimmer.   
    ​-**MA Dimmer Network** opens.

![](../../img/window_network-dimmer_normal-view_v3-3.png)

_Open MA Dimmer Network_

2.  To navigate down in the hierarchy of the dimmer network:  
    \-Tap a rack and a window displaying crates opens.
    
    ![](../../img/window_network-dimmer_crates_v3-3.png)
    
    _Navigate down to the crates_
    
      
    \-Tap a crate and a window displaying modules opens.
    
    ![](../../img/window_network-dimmer_modules_v3-3.png)
    
    _Navigate down to the modules_
    
      
    \-Tap a module and a window displaying dimmers opens.
    
    ![](../../img/window_network-dimmer_dimmers_v3-3.png)
    
    _Navigate down to the dimmers_
    

**Hint:**  
Dimmer is the deepest you can go in the hierarchy.

3.  To navigate upward in the hierarchy, tap ![](../../img/img_arrow-up_dimmer-network_v3-3.png) in the upper right corner of the title bar. 

* * *

## The dimMA Hierarchy

The dimMA hierarchy consists of racks, crates, modules, and dimmers. 

**Rack:**

A rack consists of 1 or 2 NDPs and at least 1 crate.

**Crate:**

A crate consists of 3 modules. 

**Module:**

A module consists of 1, 2 or 4 dimmers.

**Dimmer:** 

A dimmer holds all the corresponding configuration data.
