# Connect Power

**Warning:**  
If only the powerCon connector is provided, you need to attach a suitable cable to the connector.  
To attach a suitable cable to the powerCon connector, contact your local dealer.

1.  Insert the blue powerCon connector in the powerCon plug and twist it to lock 90 degrees clockwise.
2.  Connect the other end of the cable to a suitable power source.

The console is connected to power.
