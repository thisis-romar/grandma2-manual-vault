# Connect Desk Lamps

The console comes with two MA LED desk lamps.

*   Connect the 4 pin XLR connector to the desk lamp connectors on the rear panel.

The desk lamps are connected.

![](../../img/img_grandma2_desklampconnector_3_2.png)

_grandMA2 full-size desk lamp connectors_

To adjust the intensity of the desk lamp see [[Pages/Console Settings/Adjust Intensity of Desk Lights|adjust desk lights]].
