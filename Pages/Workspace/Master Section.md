# Master Section

The master section is located in the corner on the left of screen 2.

It consists of three areas.

![](../../img/img_master_section_3_2.png)

_Areas in the Master Section_ 

1.  [Selected Executor](<#Selected Executor>)
2.  [Function of the Left 100 mm Fader](<#Fader Function>)
3.  [Function of the Right 100 mm Fader](<#Fader Function>)

The master section is also displayed in the Command Section View along with [Program Time](Time%20Control.md) and [Exec Time](Time%20Control.md).

*   Open the Command Section View via the **pop-up Create Basic Window**. For more information see [Create and Manage Basic Windows](../Windows/Create%20and%20Manage%20Basic%20Windows.md). 

![](../../img/window_command-section-view_v3-3.png)

Open the Command Section View

* * *

## Selected Executor

The selected executor has two areas.

![](../../img/img_master_section_selected_executor_3_2.png)

_Selected Executor Areas_

1.  Sequence
2.  Cue

If you tap at the sequence area, the [assign menu](../Executors/Assign%20a%20function.md) opens.  
If you tap at the cue area, the [edit menu](../Command%20Syntax%20and%20Keywords/Looking%20at%20the%20cue%20content.md) opens.

For more information, see [Mini Executor Bar](Mini%20executor%20bar.md).

* * *

## Fader Function

The two faders display the current fader function of the 100 mm faders.

You can move the slider up or down and tap at the function buttons right beside the slider.

If you tap in the fader description field, the fader assignment opens.

![](../../img/popup_fader_assignment_3_2.png)

_Fader Assignment_

For more information, see [executor - assign a function](../Executors/Assign%20a%20function.md).
