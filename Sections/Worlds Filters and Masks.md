# Worlds, Filters and Masks

The grandMA2 have three different functions that can help you work faster and more efficient.

They are called Worlds, Filters, and Masks.

[Worlds](key_wfm_what_are_worlds.html) are used to limit the access to fixtures and attributes.

[Filters](key_wfm_what_are_filters.html) are used to filter attribute values when doing different actions.

[Masks](key_wfm_what_are_masks.html) are used to hide fixtures and attributes from your views.
