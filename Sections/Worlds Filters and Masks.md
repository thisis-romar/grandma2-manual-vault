# Worlds, Filters and Masks

The grandMA2 have three different functions that can help you work faster and more efficient.

They are called Worlds, Filters, and Masks.

[Worlds](../Pages/Worlds%20Filters%20and%20Masks/What%20are%20Worlds.md) are used to limit the access to fixtures and attributes.

[Filters](../Pages/Worlds%20Filters%20and%20Masks/What%20are%20filters.md) are used to filter attribute values when doing different actions.

[Masks](../Pages/Worlds%20Filters%20and%20Masks/What%20are%20masks.md) are used to hide fixtures and attributes from your views.
