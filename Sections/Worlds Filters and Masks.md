# Worlds, Filters and Masks

The grandMA2 have three different functions that can help you work faster and more efficient.

They are called Worlds, Filters, and Masks.

[[Pages/Worlds Filters and Masks/What are Worlds|Worlds]] are used to limit the access to fixtures and attributes.

[[Pages/Worlds Filters and Masks/What are filters|Filters]] are used to filter attribute values when doing different actions.

[[Pages/Worlds Filters and Masks/What are masks|Masks]] are used to hide fixtures and attributes from your views.
