# Cues and Sequences

This section is about creating, editing and viewing the cues and sequences. This is some of the primary functions of a lighting console.

The topics here describe all the basic functions and operations regarding cues and sequences. Sequences are closely related to Executors. Taking a look at the [Executors section](Executors.md) might also be a good idea.

The more advanced options and functions for sequences are described in the [Advanced Sequence Functionality section](Advanced%20Sequence%20Functionality.md). For instance this section only describes the most used elements in the Sequence Executor Sheet - the advanced section describes the rest.
