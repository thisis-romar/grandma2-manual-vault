# Safety Information

This device is conform to the following harmonized standards:

*   EN 60065 (Safety Requirements) ,
*   EN 60950-1 (Information Technology Equipment Safety)
*   EN 55103-1:2009 (Electromagnetic Compatibility)
*   EN 55103-2:2009 (Electromagnetic Compatibility)
*   2014/30/EG (Electromagnetic Compatibility)
*   2011/65/EU (RoHS II)
*   2014/35/EU (Low Voltage Directive)

This device is state-of-the-art technology.

However, residual risks cannot be ruled out.

To avoid risks, follow the safety instructions.  
MA Lighting does not cover damages which can be the result of an inobservance of the safety instructions.
