# Assign Key

The key Assign is located in the command area on the right of the key Prvw.

![](../../img/img_assign_key_3_2.png)

_Location k__ey_ _Assign_ 

*   Pressing Assign enters [Assign keyword](../Keywords/Assign%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Assign

*   Pressing Assign Assign enters [Label keyword](../Keywords/Label.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Label

*   Pressing Assign Assign Assign enters [Appearance keyword](../Keywords/Appearance%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Appearance
