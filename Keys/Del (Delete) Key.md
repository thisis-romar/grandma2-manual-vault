# Del (Delete) Key

The key Del (= Delete) is located in the command area on the right of the key Goto.

![](../../img/img_del_key_3_2.png)

_Location key Delete_ 

Pressing Del enters the [Delete keyword](../Keywords/Delete%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Delete

Pressing Del Del enters the [Remove keyword](../Keywords/Remove.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Remove

Pressing Del Del Del enters the [Release keyword](../Keywords/Release.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Release
