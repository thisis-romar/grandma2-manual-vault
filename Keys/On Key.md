# On Key

The key On is located in the command area on the right of the key Top.

![](../../img/img_on-key_v3-2.png)

_Location k__ey_ _On_ 

*   Pressing On enters the [On keyword](../Keywords/On.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> On

*   Pressing On On enters the [Call keyword](../Keywords/Call%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Call
