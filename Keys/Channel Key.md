# Channel Key

The key Channel is located on the left of the key Fixture.

![](../../img/img_channel_key_3_2.png)

_Location k__ey_ _Channel_ 

  
 

*   Pressing Channel enters the [Channel keyword](../Keywords/Channel%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Channel

*   Pressing Channel Channel enters the [Dmx keyword](../Keywords/Dmx%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Dmx

*   Pressing Channel Channel Channel enters the [DmxUniverse keyword](../Keywords/DmxUniverse%20keyword.md) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> DmxUniverse
