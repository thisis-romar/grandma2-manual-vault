# Down Key

The key Down is located in the command area below the key Set.

![](../../img/img_down-key_v3-2.png)

_Location k__ey_ _Down_ 

*   Pressing Down moves the scroll bar of windows or the focus downward.
*   Pressing and holding ![ma](../../img/ma_1.png) + Down moves the monitor wing of the console downward.
