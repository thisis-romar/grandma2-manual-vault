# V1 - V10 Keys

The keys V1 through V10 are located on the right of screen 2.

![](../../img/img_v_keys_3_2.png)

_Location V-Keys_

  
If you press a V-Key, the [ViewButton keyword](../Keywords/ViewButton.md) will be executed.

The displayed view on the [view button](../Pages/Workspace/View%20Buttons.md) will be called.

If you press and hold ![ma](../../img/ma_1.png) and a V-Key, the [ViewPage keyword](../Keywords/ViewPage.md) will be executed.  
The view page corresponding the V-Key number you have pressed, will be called.
