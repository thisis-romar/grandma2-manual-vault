# . \[Dot\] Key

The key . is located in the command area on the left of the key If.

![](../../img/img_dot-key_v3-2.png)

_Location k__ey_ _. \[dot\]_ 

*   Pressing . enters the . into the command line. The . is used as a separator to enter numbers with decimal fractions or to enter hierarchical object IDs.

\[Channel\]> .

*   Pressing . . executes the [Zero keyword](key_keyword_zero.html). The dimmer values of the selected fixtures are set to 0 %.  
     
*   Pressing and holding ![ma](../../img/ma_1.png) + . enters the [Default keyword](key_keyword_default.html) into the command line.

\[Channel\]> Default
