# X1 - X20 Keys

The X-Keys are located below screen 1.

**Information:**  
The grandMA2 ultra-light has only X1 - X10.

![](../../img/img_x_keys_3_2.png)

_Location X-Key_

If you press User1 or User2, the X-Keys will work just like [view buttons](../Pages/Workspace/View%20Buttons.md).

If you press List, the X-Keys will work just like [executor buttons](Executor%20Buttons.md).
